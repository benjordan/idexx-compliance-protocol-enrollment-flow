Program Enrollment Terms & Conditions

Effective as of November 1, 2019

Compliance Protocols (the “platform”) is designed to enable your practice to send targeted communications to your customers and to gather insight into the clinical and commercial effectiveness of those communications. Using data extracted from your practice management software, IDEXX will produce and send real-time, personalized email and or SMS messages on your practice’s behalf for the programs you select which may include flea and tick control, heartworm preventative, dental and weight management and financial products (the “programs”).

The messages will include discounts on the products that you recommend in your e-commerce store. IDEXX will provide you resultant reporting on the number and effectiveness of messages sent, and the amount of revenue generated from the programs.

By completing the platform enrollment agreement and accepting IDEXX’s End User License Agreement for its data extraction software, your practice authorizes IDEXX to periodically pull data from your practice management software and to use that data to implement programs on the platform.

In order to implement a program, IDEXX will need access to the following types of data: pet owner (“Client”) name, email address, SMS preference and SMS phone number of Client ; canine and feline pet demographics (ex: name, breed, date of birth, weight); purchases and results of IDEXX diagnostic tests; purchases of products or services that are required to support the program’s practice communications (including flea/tick, heartworm and combination preventive products and dental products and services) (together, the “Information”)

IDEXX will share the subset of the Information with your current e-commerce partner (ie, VetSource, Hills to Home, etc.) to enable your customer’s purchase experience.

By enrolling the platform, you represent that (i) you are authorized to make business decisions on behalf of your practice and authorized to enroll the practice in programs on the platform, and (ii) you have received and maintain records of written consent from each Client that allows you to use and share the Client’s email address(es) and SMS numbers for the purpose of sending commercial or marketing messages.
