Special Invitation to Our Pilot Programs!

Your practice is one of a handful that has been invited to participate in 3 new pilot programs designed to

  • Improve patient outcomes due to higher adherence with practice protocols.
  • Increase purchases of key wellness products due to “set it and forget it” auto-ship subscriptions.
  • Create a simple way to track pet parent adherence to your product recommendations.

Let us know which programs you are interested in by filling out the information below!

Canine Dental

(launches November 2019)
View Program Email
We'll send a personalized next steps email about pets who have just completed a dental cleaning to their parents at check out. It will include an immediate opportunity to purchase OraVet from your clinic's online pharmacy, on autoship at a discount from BI.

Canine Wellness

(launches December 2019)
View Program Email
Celebrate the negative! When a pet gets a negative result on a Snap 4DX test, their owners will receive a personalized message encouraging them to remain vigilant in the protection against flea and tick borne diseases. It will include an immediate opportunity to purchase NexGard and HeartGard from your clinic's online pharmacy, on autoship at a discount from BI.

Canine/Feline Obesity

(launches January 2020)
View Program Email
It's no fun talking to owners about obese pets. Let our program do the talking for you. This personalized email gets sent when a canine's BCS is 4 or 5 on a 5 point scale and 7-9 on a 9 point scale. It will include an immediate opportunity to purchase Metabolic Prescription Diet from your clinic's online pharmacy, on autoship at a discount from Hills.
